Future Goals and Aspirations

After checking out my brief intro about me and my past experiences in the computing area, you may have noticed that much of my experience was actually in Electrical engineering first rather than a software engineer, IT major or computer science background. Nevertheless, even with the various skills and exposures i've had to other languages, I still chose to undertake this introductory course as I have always felt that having a fundamental grasp of any core skill is the most significant in a person's development. This applies especially to programming, and with this subject providing insight into both the core of frontend development in the form of HTML and CSS knowledge as well a deeper comprehension of Linux based OS's and the general CLI in Bash, I was easily convinced to jump at the opportunity to polish my skills from their core.

With my skills, I have already achieved quite a successful journey in my eyes, having been granted an opportunity to join the Suncorp Group's graduate program as a graduate software engineer, and provide development knowledge and experience to their ever growing list of products.

Even with this however, I am striving for more opportunities in the near future especially if possible within the field of electrical engineering still, more specifically in the following areas:

  • Embedded Software Development
  • Renewable Energy System Programming and planning
  • Electronics Development

Given my current path of software development, I am hoping to achieve a position by which my technical skills could be polished to an extent whereby I am confident in developing in multiple different frameworks and languages with relative ease, whilst eventually having a comprehensive understanding of Dev Ops skills and general knowledge to lead as a team leader or lead developer.
